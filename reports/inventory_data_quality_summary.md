# Inventory Data Quality Summary Report

## Executive Summary

A data quality and inventory validation review was conducted on a simulated pharmacy inventory dataset.

The analysis focused on identifying operational inconsistencies, stock risks, and reporting issues that could affect inventory reliability and business operations.

---

## Key Findings

### Inventory Status

* Most inventory items were active and operational.
* A small number of inactive and expired products were identified.

### Low Stock Risks

Several products showed critically low inventory levels and may require replenishment monitoring to avoid stock shortages.

### Data Quality Issues

The following data quality issues were detected:

* Duplicate medicine identifiers
* Missing supplier information
* Invalid expiration date formats
* Inconsistent category naming
* Missing storage condition information

---

## Dashboard Insights

The Power BI dashboard provides:

* Inventory status overview
* Product category distribution
* Low stock monitoring
* Inventory KPI visibility
* Operational reporting support

---

## Recommendations

* Standardize category naming conventions
* Implement inventory validation rules
* Improve supplier data completeness
* Introduce automated stock threshold alerts
* Apply consistent expiration date formatting

---

## Conclusion

The project demonstrates how structured data quality checks and reporting workflows can support more reliable inventory management and operational visibility within pharmacy-related business environments.
